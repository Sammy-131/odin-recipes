JOLLOF RICE RECIPE

Jollof rice description

Jollof rice is a dish native to west African countries such as Nigeria, Ghana and Senegal

Ingredients

  • Tomato paste
  • Chicken stock
  • White rice
  • Bay leaves
  • Butter
  • Thyme
  • Red Onion
  • Chicken bouillon
  • Tomato
  • Red bell pepper
  • Salt
  • Garlic Powder
  • Scotch bonnet pepper
  • Tomato puree

Steps for cooking

  1. Roast your tomatoes, bell peppers, Scotch bonnet peppers, and onions in the oven
  2. wash your rice until the water runs clear
  3. blend your roasted vegetables
  4. take your tomato puree and tomato paste and fry in a pot
  5. add your blended vegetables into the pot and cook until it doesnt smell raw
  6. add your washed rice (with salt) into the pot and add your chicken stock and a little water and cook on medium-low heat
  7. cook until rice is soft (add more water and chicken stock is it isnt done)
  8. Serve with Chicken and platain, either way it's gonna taste amazing
